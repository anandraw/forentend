What is an Aptitude Test?

Aptitude tests are designed to measure a person's potential in various skills such as problem-solving, logical reasoning, and data interpretation. They are widely used in job assessments, competitive exams, and academic evaluations to identify strengths and areas of improvement.

Types of Aptitude Tests

Numerical

Assesses mathematical ability, data interpretation, and number series.

Logical

Evaluates reasoning patterns, sequence solving, and logic puzzles.

Verbal

Measures comprehension, vocabulary, and grammar proficiency.

Preparation Tips

Practice Daily

Build consistency to improve accuracy and speed.

Focus on Fundamentals

Solid understanding of basics in maths and logic is essential.

Time Management

Allocate time wisely to avoid getting stuck on a single question.
